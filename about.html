About Me

Hi I'm Christopher. I'm a senior at Marlboro High School and developer building playful, motion-forward web experiences inspired by game UI and editorial layouts. I enjoy creating interactive prototypes, visual systems, and responsive sites that balance aesthetics with usability.

My projects range from small games and galleries to recruitment and movie gallery sites. I like experimenting with layout, typography, and subtle motion to bring interfaces to life.

Outside of web work I enjoy music, photography, and exploring UI systems inspired by consoles and product design.
